EchoChat

Visualized Chat History - Revisit. Remember. Reconnect.

Digital conversations contain countless memories that get buried in endless chat histories, making it difficult to revisit meaningful moments. As relationships face challenges from distance and busy schedules, these buried memories represent lost opportunities for connection.

To bridge this gap, our solution brings past conversations back to life in a meaningful way. By surfacing shared moments and offering thoughtful prompts, we make it easier to reconnect, relive memories, and strengthen relationships—no matter the distance or schedule.
